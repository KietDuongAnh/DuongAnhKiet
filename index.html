Team Profile

I always prioritize member cooperation in order to form an effective and harmonious working group. As a result, I decided on "Amigos" as the group name because it means "friends" in Spanish. It embodies the spirit of the entire team, which is constantly assisting one another in order to progress together.

My personality test summary

·       Personality test: Logistician

·       Learning style test: Auditory and Visual

·       Creativity test: Openness, Conscientiousness and Extraversion

Teammates

Team leader:

Based on my test results and self-reflection, I could be a person who works hard and gets the job done satisfactorily. However, I have come to realize that my inflexible working style sometimes prevents me from having the wisdom to make the right decisions in difficult situations. As a result, I am hoping to find a leader who can help me overcome my weaknesses. The leader is in charge of developing a project plan, assign tasks to other team members, and provide regular updates. This person is also responsible for engaging the team members, which is a crucial factor affecting the team's effectiveness. Because the top priority is group cooperation, I am hoping for an insightful and inspiring leader. In practice, this person is able to provide a clear vision of what team members can and should do to improve themselves as well as improve working efficiency. He or she may have a positive influence on others by mesmerizing their listeners with their speech and inspiring others to do the right thing. As a result, this leader will help the team maximize its potential and guide the further development of the group.

Team members:

Besides a reliable leader, any project's heart and soul rests with its team members. In addition to being familiar with some basic programming languages such as Python, Java and C and their relevant programming skills, I am looking forward to find teammates with soft skills such as problem-solving skills and interpersonal skills, particularly the ability to collaborate effectively with others. These are who contribute to overall project objectives and complete individual deliverables responsibly. According to the personality test, I might be a practical and perceptive person, who tends to think logically and be open-minded to other viewpoints. My communication skills, on the other hand, need to be improved because I sometimes have trouble expressing myself clearly. Therefore, truthfulness and assertiveness are among the values I look for my teammates. In particular, they can speak up when something strikes them as unjust or wrong and keep the processes can run smoothly.
